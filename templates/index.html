But is it healthy?

The magic behind the heat is capsaicin, an ingredient that’s found in one of the most heavily consumed spices throughout the world: chili pepper. About a century ago, scientists began researching capsaicin from chili peppers and found that it has many surprising health benefits. Here are four advantages to adding some spice to your life:
